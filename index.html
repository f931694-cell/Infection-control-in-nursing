About Infection Control

This academic platform is dedicated to promoting safety and compliance with infection control standards in nursing practice.

  • Protects patients and healthcare providers.
  • Reduces the spread of infections (HAIs).
  • Ensures a sterilized healthcare environment.

Key Guidelines

1. Hand Hygiene

  • Performed before and after patient contact.
  • Essential for preventing the spread of germs.

2. Personal Protective Equipment (PPE)

  • Proper use of Gloves, Gowns, and Masks based on risk.
