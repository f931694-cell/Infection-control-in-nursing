امتثال الممرضين | Nurses’ Compliance

Compliance refers to how well nurses follow infection control guidelines.

Factors affecting compliance:

  • Lack of training
  • Work overload
  • Limited resources

Ways to improve compliance:

  • Continuous education and training
  • Monitoring and evaluation
  • Availability of supplies

التقييم الذاتي | Self-Assessment

Ask yourself:

  • Do I perform hand hygiene regularly?
  • Do I use PPE correctly?
  • Do I follow infection control protocols?
