About The Project

Infection control is a fundamental aspect of nursing care designed to prevent the spread of microorganisms in healthcare settings. This platform provides essential resources for nursing students to maintain a safe clinical environment.

Core Guidelines

1. Hand Hygiene

  • Wash hands for 40-60 seconds with soap and water.
  • Use alcohol-based hand rub for 20-30 seconds.

2. Personal Protective Equipment (PPE)

  • Donning Sequence: Gown → Mask → Goggles → Gloves.
  • Doffing Sequence: Gloves → Goggles → Gown → Mask.
