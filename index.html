About Infection Control

This comprehensive academic platform is dedicated to promoting safety, education, and compliance with infection control standards in nursing practice. Safety is our priority.

  • Protects patients and healthcare providers from hospital-acquired infections (HAIs).
  • Reduces microbial transmission through standardized practices.
  • Improves healthcare safety standards for a specialized workforce.

Key Guidelines

Hand Hygiene

  • Performed before and after patient contact.
  • Uses alcohol-based rub if hands are not visibly soiled.

Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) Usage

  • Follow the proper sequence for Donning (Gown → Mask → Goggles → Gloves) and Doffing.
