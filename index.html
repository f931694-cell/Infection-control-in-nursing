Welcome

This platform is designed to support nurses in preventing and controlling infections in healthcare settings to ensure patient and staff safety.

"Proper adherence to guidelines helps save lives and improve healthcare quality."

Guidelines

1. Hand Hygiene

  • Wash hands before and after patient contact
  • Use alcohol-based hand sanitizers

2. PPE

  • Use gloves, masks, and gowns when necessary
  • Follow standard precautions

Nurses’ Compliance

Compliance refers to how well nurses follow infection control guidelines.

Factors:

  • Lack of training
  • Work overload
  • Limited resources
